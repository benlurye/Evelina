– Elevated Italian & Mediterranean Fare in Fort Greene, Brooklyn –

Evelina is an upscale bistro in the heart of Brooklyn’s Fort Greene neighborhood. Open for dinner, lunch, and weekend brunch, we focus on fresh, beautiful ingredients, sourced both locally and from around the globe. With our dishes changing daily to reflect seasonal produce, we craft our menu with a delicate balance of timeless flavor and innovative perspective.
